Comprehensive Features

Everything you need to manage your business finances efficiently

Inventory Management

Comprehensive inventory tracking with products, units, purchases, and sales management. Real-time stock updates and automated inventory reports.

Payment Management

Track purchase payments, sales payments, and manage supplier/customer balances with automated reconciliation and payment reminders.

HR & Expenses

Complete HR management including employee records, salary processing, deductions, and comprehensive expense tracking with categorization.

Financial Reports

Generate comprehensive financial reports, journal entries, balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and custom analytics dashboards.

Multi-Currency

Support for multiple currencies with real-time exchange rate management, automatic conversions, and multi-currency reporting.

Bilingual Support

Full support for Persian/Dari languages with native RTL (Right-to-Left) interface, ensuring seamless experience for local users.
