Comprehensive Features

Everything you need to manage your business finances efficiently

AI-Powered

AI Smart Reports

Generate tables and charts from natural language. Ask questions like “monthly income for the last 6 months” or “top 10 selling products” and get instant reports with date filters. Export to Excel. Powered by AI for intelligent data analysis.

    Quick prompts: Monthly income • Sales by product • Purchase vs sale comparison • Stock status • Expenses by month

Products & Inventory

Manage products with SKU, units, purchase/sale prices, stock, barcode, and currency. Stock reports by category. Real-time inventory updates from purchases and sales.

Purchases

Record purchases with supplier, date, currency, exchange rate, and line items. Print purchase invoices. Track purchase payments and supplier balances. Additional costs on purchases.

Sales

Record sales with customer, date, currency, and line items. Print/export sale invoices. Track sales payments and customer balances. Sale additional costs. Full inventory integration.

Services

Register and manage services with free-form items (name and price). Ideal for service-based businesses alongside product sales.

Suppliers & Customers

Manage supplier and customer records. Link to purchases and sales. Customer and supplier detail pages with full transaction history and balance tracking.

Multi-Currency

Define currencies with exchange rates. Auto-fill rate in purchases and sales. Multi-currency reporting and conversions.

Expenses

Record expenses with amount, date, and expense type. Categorize with expense types. Full expense tracking and reporting.

HR & Payroll

Manage employees, salary records, and deductions. Link deductions to employees or use generally. Complete payroll support.

Accounts & Journal

Chart of accounts (COA) and journal entries with debit/credit. Full double-entry accounting for accurate financial records.

Financial Reports

Generate reports with date filters. Stock reports, financial analytics, and export to Excel. Comprehensive reporting suite.

Invoicing

Print and export sale and purchase invoices. Professional layouts with company logo, tax ID, and custom settings.

Company & Users

Company settings (name, address, logo, tax ID) for invoices and reports. User management and profile. Role-based access.

Backup & Restore

Save a copy of your SQLite database anytime. Restore from backup. Optional automatic daily backup to a folder of your choice.

Bilingual & RTL

Full Persian/Dari support with native Right-to-Left interface. Localized numbers and dates. IRANSans and custom fonts.

Online & Offline

Work with or without internet. Full functionality offline. Data stored locally. Optional sounds and light/dark theme.
